Quesadilla Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Quesadilla Shells
  • Bruschetta
  • Pesto
  • Cooked Chicken Strips
  • Spinach
  • Grated Mozzarella Cheese
  • Paprika

Steps:

  1. Lay out the quesadilla shells and apply pesto to the upper side
  2. Place a handful of bruschetta
  3. Place a right amount of cooked chicken strips
  4. Place a handful of spinach
  5. Top them off with plenty of grated mozarella cheese
  6. Place another queasdilla shell on top and carefully place in a cooking tray
  7. Cook in an oven with 450 degree Celsius for 5 minutes
  8. Once cooked, slice into four pieces
  9. Sprinkle paprika on top and enjoy!
