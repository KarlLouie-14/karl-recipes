Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Rice
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Egg
  • Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Heat up the butter and saute the garlic and onions
  2. Add the cooked rice and add the seasonings
  3. Make a well in the center and crack two eggs
  4. Add more butter if necessary
  5. Once everything is fully incorporated, turn off the heat and serve. Enjoy!
