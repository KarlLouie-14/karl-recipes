Fried Chicken Recipes

When it involves chicken there simply isn’t some thing extra delicious than a piece of finger-licking correctly fried chicken. It may appear intimidating to fry your very own chicken, however, it’s clearly quite truthful and it places grocery store and fast food fried chicken to shame. If you’ve ever desired to make your very own fried chicken, now's the time to try!

Ingredients:

  • Cooking Oil
  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Heat up the cooking oil to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. While the oil is heating up, coat the chicken with flour and seasoning
  3. Carefully place the coated chicken in the hot oil and wait for 10-15 minutes
  4. Once the coating is golden brown, carefully lift out the chicken
  5. Place in a cooling rack and drain excess oil
  6. Serve with favorite sides and drinks, and enjoy!
